One Direction

One Direction was a British-Irish boy band that formed in 2010. The group consisted of Niall Horan, Liam Payne, Harry Styles, Louis Tomlinson, and previously, Zayn Malik. One Direction rose to fame after competing on the seventh season of the British singing competition show, The X Factor, and ultimately finishing in third place.
