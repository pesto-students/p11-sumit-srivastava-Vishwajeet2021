Mumbai

Location

Mumbai is located on the west coast of India, in the state of Maharashtra. It is the capital city of Maharashtra and the financial capital of India.

About

Population

As of the 2021 estimate, the population of Mumbai is approximately 20.8 million people. It is the most populous city in India and one of the most densely populated cities in the world. The city has grown rapidly over the past few decades, attracting migrants from all over India and beyond, and has become a hub of commerce, finance, and entertainment. The population density of Mumbai is around 21,000 people per square kilometer, making it a bustling and vibrant metropolis.

Sports

Mumbai has a rich sporting culture, with a strong emphasis on cricket. The city is home to the iconic Wankhede Stadium, which is a regular venue for international cricket matches. The Mumbai Indians, a franchise in the Indian Premier League (IPL), is one of the most successful teams in the league's history and has a huge following in the city. Football is also a popular sport in Mumbai, with the city hosting the Mumbai City FC team in the Indian Super League (ISL). Other sports that are played in the city include field hockey, badminton, tennis, and kabaddi. In addition to professional sports, Mumbai has a strong fitness and recreational culture, with many gyms, yoga studios, and fitness centers spread throughout the city. The city also has numerous parks and public spaces where people can engage in outdoor activities such as jogging, cycling, and playing sports.
